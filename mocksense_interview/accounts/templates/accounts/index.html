About This App

Mock Sense Interview is an innovative web application created in 2025 to help students prepare effectively for job placements and interviews. Designed by final-year students from Karpagam Institute of Technology, this platform replicates real-world interview scenarios through AI-powered tools and interactive simulations.

The app includes online aptitude & coding assessments, AI-based group discussions with emotion tracking, and technical interview simulations tailored to the user’s domain. With instant feedback and insightful analytics, this tool boosts communication, technical, and critical thinking skills.

Creators:

Mohamed Fayash, Sowmiya, Ragaswathi, Sanmugam from Karpagam Institute of Technology.
